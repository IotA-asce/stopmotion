# Stop Motion Privacy Policy

Stop Motion is designed to work offline. Your projects, frames, recordings, and project metadata are stored on your device in application-owned storage.

The app does not create an account, send project data to a server, or include analytics collection. Camera, microphone, and photo-library access are requested only when you use the related capture, recording, import, or save action.

Your project leaves the device only when you choose an export destination or share an export through your operating system. You may also choose to share a diagnostic archive for support. Diagnostic archives are designed to exclude project titles, frames, audio, media names, source paths, and other user content.

For support, use the issue tracker at https://github.com/IotA-asce/stopmotion/issues.
