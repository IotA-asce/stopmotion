# Phases 7-9 Implementation Plan

> **For Claude:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use `feature-delivery` to implement this plan task-by-task.

**Goal:** Deliver export, recovery, settings, diagnostics, accessibility, performance, privacy, and compliance capabilities required before the 1.0 release-candidate phase.

**Architecture:** Keep media, filesystem, settings, recovery, and diagnostics contracts package-independent and inject platform adapters at bootstrap. Export renders immutable project sources through the existing frame renderer, journals every operation, streams bounded-memory outputs, and records independently probeable history. Recovery and settings are repository-driven Riverpod features; Phase 9 adds reusable automated audits and release evidence without claiming physical-device results that were not measured.

**Tech Stack:** Flutter 3.44, Dart 3.12, Riverpod, Drift, FFmpegKit minimal LGPL, image, archive, file_selector, share_plus, shared_preferences, Flutter test and native Android/iOS builds.
